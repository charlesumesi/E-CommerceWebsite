Hi and welcome to my demo e-commerce site! A few words.

As I just mentioned, this is a demonstration e-commerce site I've developed for my assessors to have a look at. It does almost all the things a real e-commerce site does except process payments and deliver orders! So please don't put in any real payment card details.

Like any real e-commerce site, any 'orders' and details you send do get sent to the site's database, which I do look at. (This is what happens with real e-commerce sites otherwise online orders cannot be processed).

Now I have taken pictures from the open Web through Google searches to populate my demo site (26 of them). I do not own the copyright to any of these pictures nor do I use them to sell or provide any goods to customers. I've only applied them to demonstrate my concept (in a good way!).

If you're a copyright owner to any of the pictures, then many thanks! If you wish your picture to be taken off then the best way to indicate this is to 'buy' or 'hire' the product with the picture concerned and then post a review on the product's webpage. All reviews sent reach the site's database; I will subsequently act on that.

And one other thing, this site is not necessarily mobile-phone touchscreen sensitive; this is deliberate.

Thanks for visiting my site and I hope you enjoy (or are able to enjoy) my demo!
